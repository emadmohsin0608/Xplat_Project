Who We Are

Raised Pharma is one of the most leading Canadian health care company in Pharmaceutical Industry. We are highly qualified team members providing innovative Pharmaceutical products and premium quality natural supplement based on pharmaceutical principles. We started our journey with full dedication to offer with high-quality Pharmaceutical products and natural supplements which can be easily accessible to all. Quality is ingrained in our team to produce the best quality products. Our Pharmaceutical and Natural supplement products meet all the required global regulations, quality and compliance requirements. We understand the importance of health and therefore never compromise on any quality factors. We provide Pharmaceutical, Nutraceutical products and our products are quality guaranteed. We make sure to give 100% quality products to our potential customers. Our team follows all the ethical disciplines and brings them with best innovative solutions.

We are pride to offer a comprehensive range of consulting & Contract Research Organization (CRO) services with cost effective and within the time frame. We provide complete support to pharmaceutical & (NHP) natural supplement manufacturers and brand owners for Research & Product development, from pre-formulation to stability studies, troubleshooting, regulatory affairs, registration of products globally, analytical testing, procurement of API (Medicinal substance),Excipients, Equipment's, Tools and beyond.
